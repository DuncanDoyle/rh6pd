Welcome to the JBoss Middleware six products demonstration. If you are new to JBoss middleware this suite of simple demos will allow you to get up and running with six of the most popular JBoss products very quickly.

Essentially we will demonstrate how easy it is to build an I.T platform for selling car insurance.

Although a simple representation of the actual insurance industry process it will cover all aspects of Enterprise Architecture required to get to a fully functioning quote engine in place.

This Demo is aimed at showcasing 6 of Red Hat's JBoss Middleware products i.e

  • JBoss Enterprise Data Services Platform
  • JBoss Business Process Management
  • JBoss Business Rules Management Platform
  • JBoss Enterprise Portal Platform
  • JBoss Service Orientated Architecture Platform
  • JBoss Operations Network
